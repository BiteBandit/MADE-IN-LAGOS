Useful Tips for Players

  • Use /do to describe your character's actions.
  • Use /m to make announcements or warnings.
  • Only authorized factions like EFCC are allowed to go for raids.
  • Do not log out during an arrest — this will be treated as FRP.
  • Report hackers and rule-breakers through the WhatsApp group or report page.
  • Respect higher ranks and always respond during interactions.
  • Stay updated by following announcements from the WhatsApp group.
