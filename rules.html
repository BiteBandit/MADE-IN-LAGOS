Server Rules

1. Respect All Players
  • Be respectful to all players. Avoid any form of harassment or offensive behavior.
2. No Cheating or Exploiting Bugs (Permanent Ban)
  • Cheating or exploiting bugs to gain an unfair advantage will result in a permanent ban. Hackers are not tolerated on the server as they ruin the experience for others and damage the integrity of the game. If you're caught using any form of cheat, mod, or hack, you will be permanently banned without warning.
3. No Spamming in Chats
  • Spamming or flooding the chat with unnecessary messages is prohibited.
4. Follow the Server's Theme
  • Respect the server's theme and avoid content that doesn't align with the game's style or objective.
5. No Griefing
  • Griefing (ruining others' experiences, destroying builds, etc.) is strictly prohibited.
6. Follow the Admin's Instructions
  • Always listen to and follow the instructions of the server admins and moderators.
7. Keep Language Appropriate
  • Using offensive or inappropriate language in chat is not allowed.
8. Respect Server Events
  • Participate respectfully in events and activities organized by the server.
9. No Advertising
  • Advertising external servers, websites, or services is not allowed unless authorized by the admin team.
10. FRP (Fail Roleplay) or Logging Out During Arrest
  • If you're caught disconnecting (logging out) during an arrest or while involved in a roleplay situation, you will face strict penalties. This is considered as Fail Roleplay (FRP) and is not allowed on the server. Anyone caught engaging in FRP will be banned for a set period of time or permanently, depending on the severity of the offense.
11. Have Fun!
  • Above all, enjoy your time on the server and have fun while playing!
