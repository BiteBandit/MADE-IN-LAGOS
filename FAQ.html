Frequently Asked Questions

What is our server's main purpose?
Our server is dedicated to providing an immersive gaming experience with constant updates and strong anti-hacking measures.
How do I report a player?
You can report a player through our 'Drop a Report' page. Simply provide their in-game name and description of the incident.
How can I contact the server administrators?
You can reach out to us via the 'Contact Us' page for any suggestions, feedback, or concerns regarding the server.
How do I join the server?
You can join the server by using the IP address provided on the 'Server Info' page. Ensure you have the required game version.
What should I do if I encounter a bug or glitch in the game?
If you encounter a bug or glitch, please report it immediately through our 'Drop a Report' page with as much detail as possible.
What happens if I get banned from the server?
If you get banned, you will receive a notification explaining the reason for the ban. To appeal, contact the server admins via the 'Contact Us' page.
Can I play with friends on the server?
Yes, our server supports multiplayer gameplay. Invite your friends to join and experience the game together!
