About Our Server

The Journey

Our server has faced countless challenges since its creation, including multiple hacking attacks and betrayal attempts. Despite these, we’ve stood strong and continued growing stronger each day.

The Mysterious Fall of Fortunate

Fortunate, our former president, once held great power. But during the intense process of dethroning him, he shockingly attempted to steal server funds. His betrayal shocked the community, but it also united us to take swift action and protect our legacy.

The Rise of Big Loner

After Fortunate's removal, Big Loner rose to leadership. Calm, strategic, and focused on the players’ best interests, Big Loner has led the server into a new era of order and fairness. Under his leadership, our community thrives.

About the Server Owner - Nicky Demons

The heart and soul of the server, Nicky Demons, is a visionary with an unshakable commitment to fairness, creativity, and security. Nicky's leadership has kept the server alive through countless trials. Without him, none of this would be possible.
